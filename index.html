About

Advocate. Creator.
Changemaker.

Adv. Peehu Maheshwari is a practicing advocate and legal content creator who believes that legal awareness is a fundamental right — not a privilege. Through her content, courtroom practice and foundation, she works tirelessly to make law accessible and understandable for everyone.

As the founder of Peehu Maheshwari Foundation, she works towards legal empowerment, women's rights, and justice for the underprivileged — combining the power of advocacy with the reach of social media.

Social Impact

Peehu Maheshwari
Foundation

The Peehu Maheshwari Foundation is Adv. Peehu's commitment to making justice accessible for everyone — especially those who cannot afford it. Through the foundation, we provide free legal aid, awareness programs and advocacy for marginalised communities.

From women's rights to children's education — the foundation works at the intersection of law and social change, driven by the belief that every person deserves access to justice.
